DEPLOYMENT PATTERNS SUMMARY
===========================

1. STANDALONE (Best for: Dev, Testing, Small Scale)
   Files: production.yaml, Dockerfile, docker-compose.yaml, prometheus.yml
   Setup Time: 10 minutes
   Complexity: Low
   
   Quick Start:
   $ cd deployments/standalone/docker
   $ docker-compose up -d
   $ curl http://localhost:8080/health

2. SIDECAR (Best for: Kubernetes, Microservices)
   Files: deployment.yaml
   Setup Time: 30 minutes
   Complexity: Medium
   
   Quick Start:
   $ kubectl apply -f deployments/sidecar/kubernetes/deployment.yaml
   $ kubectl get pods -n llm-edge-agent

3. SERVICE MESH (Best for: Enterprise, Large Scale)
   Files: envoy-filter.yaml
   Setup Time: 1-2 hours
   Complexity: High
   
   Quick Start:
   $ kubectl apply -f deployments/service-mesh/istio/envoy-filter.yaml
   $ istioctl analyze
